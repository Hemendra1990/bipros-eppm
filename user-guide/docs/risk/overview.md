# Risk Management

The **Risk Management** module provides an enterprise-wide view of risks across all projects. It complements the project-level risk analysis by aggregating risks into a single register for portfolio-level oversight.

## Accessing Risk Management

Click **Risk** in the sidebar.

![Risk Management](/img/screenshots/08-risk.png)

## Enterprise Risk Register

The enterprise risk register consolidates all risks from all projects:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Risk ID** | Unique identifier |
| **Project** | The project this risk belongs to |
| **Description** | What could go wrong |
| **Category** | Classification (Technical, Schedule, Cost, Environmental, Legal, Safety) |
| **Probability** | Likelihood of occurrence: Low (1), Medium (2), High (3) |
| **Impact** | Severity: Low (1), Medium (2), High (3), Critical (4) |
| **Risk Score** | Probability × Impact — used for ranking |
| **Owner** | Person responsible for managing this risk |
| **Mitigation** | Planned response actions |
| **Status** | Open, Mitigated, Closed, Realized |

## Risk Categories

| Category | Description | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Technical** | Risks related to design, technology, or construction methods | Unforeseen ground conditions, design errors |
| **Schedule** | Risks that may cause delays | Weather delays, permit delays, resource shortages |
| **Cost** | Risks that may cause cost overruns | Material price escalation, scope changes |
| **Environmental** | Environmental compliance risks | Clearance delays, environmental incidents |
| **Legal** | Contractual or regulatory risks | Contractual disputes, regulatory changes |
| **Safety** | Workplace safety risks | Accidents, occupational hazards |

## Risk Matrix

Risks are assessed using a **Probability × Impact** matrix:

| | Low Impact (1) | Medium Impact (2) | High Impact (3) | Critical Impact (4) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **High Probability (3)** | 3 - Medium | 6 - High | 9 - Critical | 12 - Critical |
| **Medium Probability (2)** | 2 - Low | 4 - Medium | 6 - High | 8 - High |
| **Low Probability (1)** | 1 - Low | 2 - Low | 3 - Medium | 4 - Medium |

## Risk Response Strategies

| Strategy | When to Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Avoid** | Eliminate the risk entirely | Change the design to avoid unforeseen ground conditions |
| **Mitigate** | Reduce probability or impact | Add ground investigation to reduce uncertainty |
| **Transfer** | Shift risk to another party | Include risk in contractor's scope via contract clauses |
| **Accept** | Acknowledge and monitor | Set aside contingency budget for minor price fluctuations |

## Abbreviations

| Abbreviation | Full Form |
|---|---|
| P × I | Probability × Impact |
