# Glossary & Abbreviations

This page provides a comprehensive reference of all abbreviations, acronyms, and technical terms used throughout Bipros EPPM.

---

## A

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **AC** | Actual Cost | The total cost actually incurred for the work performed during a specific time period |
| **API** | Application Programming Interface | A set of protocols for building and integrating application software |

## B

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **BAC** | Budget at Completion | The total authorized budget for the entire project |
| **BOQ** | Bill of Quantities | A document listing the quantities of materials, labour, and equipment required |

## C

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **CPI** | Cost Performance Index | A measure of cost efficiency: CPI = EV / AC. Values above 1.0 indicate under-budget performance |
| **CPM** | Critical Path Method | A scheduling algorithm that calculates the longest sequence of dependent activities and determines the minimum project duration |
| **CPPP** | Central Public Procurement Portal | India's central government procurement portal for publishing tenders and managing bids |
| **cum** | Cubic Metres | Unit of measurement for volume (concrete, earthwork, sand, etc.) |
| **CV** | Cost Variance | The difference between earned value and actual cost: CV = EV - AC |

## D

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **DLP** | Defect Liability Period | The period after practical completion during which the contractor must rectify any defects at their own cost. Typically 12-24 months. |

## E

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **EAC** | Estimate at Completion | Forecast of the total cost of the project based on current performance: EAC = BAC / CPI |
| **EF** | Early Finish | The earliest date an activity can finish, calculated by the CPM forward pass |
| **EPPM** | Enterprise Project Portfolio Management | A system for managing multiple projects across an enterprise, integrating scheduling, cost, resource, and risk management |
| **EPS** | Enterprise Project Structure | The top-level hierarchical structure that organizes all projects within an enterprise |
| **ES** | Early Start | The earliest date an activity can start, calculated by the CPM forward pass |
| **ETC** | Estimate to Complete | The expected cost to finish the remaining work: ETC = EAC - AC |
| **EV** | Earned Value | The value of work actually completed, calculated as budget multiplied by actual completion percentage |
| **EVM** | Earned Value Management | A project management methodology that integrates scope, schedule, and cost data to measure project performance |

## F

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **FF** | Free Float | The amount of time an activity can be delayed without delaying the early start of any successor activity |
| **FF (Relationship)** | Finish-to-Finish | A dependency relationship where the successor cannot finish until the predecessor finishes |
| **FS** | Finish-to-Start | A dependency relationship where the successor cannot start until the predecessor finishes. The most common relationship type. |

## G

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **GeM** | Government e-Marketplace | India's government procurement platform for online purchasing of goods and services |
| **GIS** | Geographic Information System | A system for capturing, storing, analysing, and presenting spatial or geographic data |
| **GST** | Goods and Services Tax | India's indirect tax on the supply of goods and services |
| **GSTN** | Goods and Services Tax Network | The IT backbone managing the GST system in India, used for taxpayer verification |

## I

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **ICT** | Information and Communication Technology | The technology infrastructure sector including telecommunications, computing, and networking |

## J

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **JWT** | JSON Web Token | A compact, URL-safe means of representing claims to be transferred between two parties, used for authentication |

## K

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **KPI** | Key Performance Indicator | A measurable value that demonstrates how effectively objectives are being achieved |

## L

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **LD** | Liquidated Damages | Pre-agreed monetary compensation payable by the contractor for each day of delay beyond the contractual completion date |
| **LF** | Late Finish | The latest date an activity can finish without delaying the project, calculated by the CPM backward pass |
| **LOA** | Letter of Award | The formal document issued to a contractor awarding them the contract |
| **LS** | Late Start | The latest date an activity can start without delaying the project, calculated by the CPM backward pass |
| **Ltrs** | Litres | Unit of measurement for liquid volume (fuel, paint, admixtures) |

## M

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **MEP** | Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing | Building services engineering disciplines |
| **MT** | Metric Tonnes | Unit of mass equal to 1,000 kilograms, used for steel, cement, and aggregates |

## N

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **Nos** | Numbers | Unit of measurement for countable items (precast units, fixtures, fittings) |

## O

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **OAuth** | Open Authorization | An open standard for access delegation, commonly used for token-based authentication |
| **OBS** | Organizational Breakdown Structure | A hierarchical representation of an organization's structure used to assign responsibility for project work |

## P

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **P50** | 50th Percentile | In Monte Carlo simulation, the project finish date with a 50% probability of being met |
| **P80** | 80th Percentile | In Monte Carlo simulation, the project finish date with an 80% probability of being met |
| **P90** | 90th Percentile | In Monte Carlo simulation, the project finish date with a 90% probability of being met |
| **PARIVESH** | PARIVESH Portal | India's single-window environmental clearance portal managed by the Ministry of Environment |
| **PFMS** | Public Financial Management System | India's government financial management and payment platform operated by the Controller General of Accounts |
| **PMO** | Programme Management Office | The organizational unit responsible for overseeing programme-level governance and coordination |
| **PV** | Planned Value | The authorized budget assigned to scheduled work — the value of work that was planned to be completed by a given date |

## R

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **RA** | Running Account | A periodic payment mechanism where contractors submit bills for work completed during each billing period |
| **RFI** | Request for Information | A formal question raised during a project when clarity is needed on drawings, specifications, or scope |
| **RMT** | Running Metres | Unit of linear measurement for pipes, cables, kerb stones, and similar linear elements |

## S

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **SF** | Start-to-Finish | A dependency relationship where the successor cannot finish until the predecessor starts. Rarely used. |
| **SPI** | Schedule Performance Index | A measure of schedule efficiency: SPI = EV / PV. Values above 1.0 indicate ahead-of-schedule performance |
| **sqm** | Square Metres | Unit of measurement for area (formwork, flooring, painting, waterproofing) |
| **SS** | Start-to-Start | A dependency relationship where the successor cannot start until the predecessor starts |
| **SV** | Schedule Variance | The difference between earned value and planned value: SV = EV - PV |

## T

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **T&M** | Time and Material | A contract type where the client pays for time spent and materials used, rather than a fixed price |
| **TDS** | Tax Deducted at Source | Tax withheld from payments to contractors as required by Indian tax regulations |
| **TF** | Total Float | The amount of time an activity can be delayed without delaying the project finish date. Activities with TF = 0 are on the critical path. |

## V

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **VAC** | Variance at Completion | The expected cost variance at the end of the project: VAC = BAC - EAC |

## W

| Term | Full Form | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **WBS** | Work Breakdown Structure | A hierarchical decomposition of the total scope of a project into manageable work packages and deliverables |
