# Analytics

The **Analytics** module provides an AI-powered natural-language interface for querying project data. Instead of navigating through multiple screens and reports, you can ask questions in plain English and receive instant answers.

## Accessing Analytics

Click **Analytics** in the sidebar.

![Analytics](/img/screenshots/09-analytics.png)

## How It Works

1. Type a question in the text input field at the top of the page
2. The AI analyses your project data and generates a response
3. Responses may include text, tables, charts, or summary cards
4. Your query history is maintained for the current session

## Suggested Queries

The Analytics page provides suggested queries to get you started:

| Suggested Query | What It Returns |
|---|---|
| **"What is the cost overrun risk?"** | Identifies projects with CPI below 1.0 and estimates the magnitude of cost overrun |
| **"Which projects are delayed?"** | Lists projects where SPI below 1.0 or activities are overdue |
| **"Show schedule summary"** | Displays a summary of all project schedules with completion percentages |
| **"What are the top risks?"** | Returns the highest-scored risks across the portfolio |
| **"How are resources allocated?"** | Shows resource utilization across projects |

## Example Questions You Can Ask

### Schedule Questions
- "How many activities are on the critical path?"
- "What is the project's expected completion date?"
- "Which activities are overdue?"
- "Show the look-ahead for the next 4 weeks"

### Cost Questions
- "What is the current CPI?"
- "How much has been spent vs. budgeted?"
- "What is the estimate at completion?"
- "Show cost variance by WBS element"

### Resource Questions
- "Which resources are over-allocated?"
- "How many man-days have been logged this month?"
- "Show equipment utilization rates"

### Risk Questions
- "How many open risks do we have?"
- "What are the critical risks?"
- "Show risk distribution by category"

## Query History

The module maintains a history of your queries within the current session. You can:

- **Review previous queries** and their results
- **Re-run a query** by clicking on it in the history
- **Refine questions** based on previous responses

## Tips for Better Results

- **Be specific** — "What is the CPI for Project NH-48?" gives a better answer than "How is the project doing?"
- **Reference metrics** — Use standard terms like CPI, SPI, EVM, WBS for precise answers
- **Ask follow-up questions** — Build on previous answers to drill deeper into the data

## Abbreviations

| Abbreviation | Full Form |
|---|---|
| CPI | Cost Performance Index |
| SPI | Schedule Performance Index |
| EVM | Earned Value Management |
| WBS | Work Breakdown Structure |
| AI | Artificial Intelligence |
